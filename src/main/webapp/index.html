Over Mistehan

Mistehan dedicates his life to explore the deepest corners of Disco, Soul, Funk and House music.
Proper educated by his Dutch father with vinyl records like Jimi Hendrix, Carlos Santana & Queen and also raised by his Chinese mother which gave him the curiosity to discover the endless Asian vinyl treasuries.
Follow Mistehan on his journey and discover some of the finest musical waves which will enter your ears for the very first time.
" Mistehan is simply an individual who fucking loves music"
